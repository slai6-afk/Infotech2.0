Core Ideas

Click the buttons to learn more about these terms.

Trees

Trees have roots, a trunk, branches, and leaves. Roots drink water and take nutrients from soil. Leaves help trees breathe and make oxygen for us.

Photosynthesis

Photosynthesis means plants make food using sunlight, water, and air (carbon dioxide). That is why plants need light and water to grow.

Soil

Soil holds water and nutrients. Healthy soil is not just dirt—there can be tiny living things inside. Soil helps roots stay strong and feed the plant.

Bugs

Some bugs help gardens (like bees). Some bugs hurt plants (like aphids). We should observe first, then choose a gentle way to protect the plant.
